Installing Docker

	You can go to this page from this link:
		Docker download page: https://www.docker.com/get-started/

	1. Go to google and search for "docker"
	2. Go to "get started" section
	3. Check the system requirements for docker on your computer and verify if your computer accomplish those for correct behavior.
	4. Click on "Get Docker"
	5. Select your operating system
	6. Download docker for your Operating system
	7. Execute the installer
	8. Once the installer ends, open CMD or terminal window and type the next command:

		docker --version
